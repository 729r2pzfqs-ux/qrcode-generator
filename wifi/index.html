Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a WiFi QR code?

Enter your WiFi network name (SSID), password, and security type (WPA/WPA2). Click generate and download your QR code. Anyone who scans it will automatically connect to your WiFi.

Is it safe to share WiFi via QR code?

Yes! The password is encoded in the QR code itself - we don't store your credentials. Only people with physical access to the QR code can scan and connect.

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes! Both iPhone (iOS 11+) and Android phones can scan WiFi QR codes using their built-in camera app. No special app needed.

Do you store my WiFi password?

No. Everything happens in your browser. Your WiFi credentials are never sent to any server.
