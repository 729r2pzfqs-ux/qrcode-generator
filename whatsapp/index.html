Frequently Asked Questions

Do customers need my number saved to contact me?

No! WhatsApp QR codes use wa.me links which allow anyone to start a chat with you without saving your number first.

Does this work with WhatsApp Business?

Yes! WhatsApp QR codes work with both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business accounts.

What's a good pre-filled message?

Keep it short and actionable. Examples: "Hi! I'd like to place an order" or "Hello, I have a question about..." The customer can edit before sending.

Is my phone number safe?

Your number is encoded in the QR code to create the WhatsApp link. We don't store your number. Only people who scan your QR code will see it.
