Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code for my restaurant menu?

Upload your menu to a free service like Google Drive or your website. Copy the public link and paste it here to generate a QR code. Print and place on tables.

Can I update my menu without changing the QR code?

Yes! The QR code points to your URL. Update the content at that URL (your website or PDF) and the same QR code will show the new menu.

What size should the QR code be?

At least 1 inch (2.5cm) for table placement. For posters or windows, 3-4 inches works best. Ensure good lighting and contrast.

Do customers need an app to scan?

No! All modern iPhones and Android phones can scan QR codes with their built-in camera app. No special app needed.
