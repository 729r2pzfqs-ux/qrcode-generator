Frequently Asked Questions

What is a vCard QR code?

A vCard QR code contains contact information (name, phone, email, company) that can be scanned and saved directly to a phone's contacts. It's like a digital business card.

How do I add it to my business card?

Download the QR code and add it to your business card design in any graphics software. When people scan it, your contact info is instantly saved to their phone.

Does it work without internet?

Yes! All the contact info is encoded in the QR code itself. No internet connection needed to save the contact.
