What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store information like URLs, text, contact details, or WiFi credentials. When scanned with a smartphone camera, it instantly opens the encoded content.

Contrast is Key

Dark QR code on light background works best. Avoid low-contrast combinations like yellow on white or light blue on light gray. Our generator uses black on white by default for maximum compatibility.

Test Before Printing

Always scan your QR code with multiple phones before printing. Test with iPhone, Android, and different QR scanner apps to ensure it works for everyone.

Leave Quiet Zone

Keep a white border (quiet zone) around your QR code ‚Äî at least the width of 4 modules (the small squares). This helps scanners detect the code edges.
