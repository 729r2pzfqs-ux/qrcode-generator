What is a WiFi QR Code?

A WiFi QR code encodes your network credentials (SSID, password, and security type) in a scannable format. When someone scans it with their phone camera, they're prompted to connect to your WiFi network ‚Äî no typing required.

How to Create a WiFi QR Code

  1. Find your WiFi details: Network name (SSID) and password. Check your router or existing connection.
  2. Choose security type: Most modern networks use WPA/WPA2. Older networks might use WEP.
  3. Generate the code: Enter details in our WiFi QR generator.
  4. Print or display: Put it somewhere visible for guests.

WiFi QR Code Security Tips

  • Use a guest network: Create a separate network for visitors to keep your main network secure.
  • Change passwords regularly: Update your QR code when you change passwords.
  • Don't share publicly: For business, keep the QR code inside the premises only.
  • Use WPA2 or WPA3: Avoid outdated WEP encryption.

How Guests Connect

The process is simple for your guests:

  1. Open camera app (iPhone) or QR scanner (Android)
  2. Point at the QR code
  3. Tap the notification that appears
  4. Confirm to join the network

Works on iOS 11+ and Android 10+. Most modern phones support this natively without any extra apps.
